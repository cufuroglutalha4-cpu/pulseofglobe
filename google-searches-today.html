Why this matters

Search is one of the clearest signals of human attention. It reflects breaking news, consumer intent, education, entertainment and problem-solving at global scale.

Attention in motion

Search behavior changes quickly when news breaks, prices move, products launch or people need answers.

Commercial intent

Many searches are connected to shopping, travel, finance, health questions and local decisions.

Knowledge demand

Search volume reveals how often people turn curiosity into action.

Digital infrastructure

Every search depends on data centers, networks, ranking systems and energy use.
