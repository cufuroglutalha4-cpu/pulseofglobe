Why this matters

AI vs human activity matters because it puts generative AI growth into context instead of treating AI output as isolated from everyday internet behavior.

Scale comparison

Searches, emails and social posts show how large human digital behavior remains compared with AI-generated output.

AI as a layer

AI is not replacing every human action; it is becoming a layer inside writing, search, work, design and communication.

Useful perspective

Comparing categories helps readers avoid both hype and dismissal when thinking about AI adoption.

Key signals to watch

These signals shape how the AI and digital activity picture changes over time.

Search and answers

AI assistants can change how people ask questions and how much text is generated before publishing.

Work automation

Email drafts, summaries, code and support workflows can shift some human activity into AI-assisted activity.

Platform integration

AI features inside social, search and productivity tools can blur the line between human and AI output.

How this dashboard works

The dashboard uses separate annual baselines for each activity and converts them into UTC-based daily progress. The comparison is directional and designed for readability.
